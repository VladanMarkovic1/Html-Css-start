Destinations

WINTER TRIPS

Zermatt

Zermatt is a picturesque alpine village nestled in the Swiss Alps, renowned for its stunning views of the Matterhorn, one of the most iconic mountains in the world. This village offers natural beauty and Swiss charm, attracting visitors year-round for skiing and snowboarding in winter.

Price: 899$/ 5 days (four nights)/ 1 person

Hotel 4 stars

Aspen

Aspen, Colorado, is a premier winter destination known for its world-class skiing and snowboarding resorts. The town also offers cultural events, fine dining, and luxury accommodations, making it a favorite among winter sports enthusiasts and travelers seeking a luxurious mountain retreat.

Price: 1200$/ 5 days (four nights)/ 1 person

Hotel 5 stars

SUMMER TRIPS

Havana

Havana, Cuba, is a vibrant and historically rich city known for its colorful architecture and vintage cars. Havana's cultural scene is diverse, featuring traditional music like son cubano and salsa. The city's charm and resilience continue to attract visitors from around the world.

Price: 399$/ 7 days (6 nights)/ 1 person

Hotel 3 stars

Mauritius

Mauritius, nestled in the Indian Ocean, is renowned for its stunning sandy beaches that attract tourists from around the globe. Visitors can explore vibrant markets and discover lush forests and waterfalls. The island's society celebrates festivals like Diwali, Eid, and Chinese New Year.

Price: 990$/ 10 days (nine nights)/ 1 person

Hotel 5 stars
