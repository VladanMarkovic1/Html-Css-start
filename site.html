Destinations

WINTER TRIPS

Zermatt

Zermatt is a picturesque alpine village

village nestled in the Swiss Alps, renowned for

its stunning views of the Matterhorn,

one of the most iconic mountains in the world.

This village offers a natural beauty and Swiss

charm, attracting visitors year-round for

skiing and snowboarding in winter.

Price: 899$/ 5 days(four nights)/ 1 person

Hotel 4 stars

Hallstatt

Hallstatt, nestled in the Austrian Alps,

is a picturesque village with a rich history.

Visitors can explore cobblestone streets,

visit the Hallstatt Museum, and take boat rides

on Lake Hallstatt for breathtaking Alpine views.

Hallstatt is a UNESCO World Heritage site.

Price:500$/ 5 days(four nights)/ 1 person

Hotel 5 stars

SUMMER TRIPS

Havana

Havana, Cuba, is a vibrant and historically

rich city known for its colorful architecture,

vintage cars.

Havana's cultural scene is diverse, featuring

traditional music like son cubano and salsa

City's charm and resilience continue to

attract visitors from around the world.

Price:399$/ 7 days(6 nights)/ 1 person

Hotel 3 stars

Mauritius

Mauritius, nestled in the Indian Ocean,

is renowned for its stunning sandy beaches

that attract tourists from around the globe.

Visitors can explore vibrant markets, and

discover lush forests and waterfalls.

The island's society celebrates festivals

like Diwali, Eid, and Chinese New Year.

Price:990$/ 10 days(9 nights)/ 1 person

Hotel 5 stars
